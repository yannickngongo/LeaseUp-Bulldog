# Cold Email Templates for LUB Outreach

Three variants below. **Use Template A for warm intros, B for cold prospects, C for re-engagement.** Test 2 at a time, kill the loser, iterate the winner.

Best send time: Tuesday or Wednesday, 9-11 AM in the prospect's timezone.

Subject lines: keep under 50 characters, lowercase, conversational. Avoid "follow up" or "?" at end (spam triggers).

## Variables to replace before sending

- `{{first_name}}` — the prospect's first name
- `{{company}}` — their property/portfolio name
- `{{X_properties}}` — number of properties they manage (research before sending)
- `{{introducer_first_name}}` — name of the person who introduced you (Template A only)
- `{{your_name}}` — your name
- `{{your_phone}}` — your phone (puts you 100x ahead of the AI-only senders)
- `{{calendly_url}}` — your booking link

## Subject line A/B test pool

Test these against the defaults. Aim for 30%+ open rate.

- "your slowest leasing problem"
- "what {{company}} is losing to slow lead response"
- "a 60-second test for {{company}}"
- "quick question about {{company}}'s leasing"
- "saw {{company}} on zillow"
- "how does {{company}} answer leads at 2am?"

---

## Things NOT to do

❌ "I hope this email finds you well" → instant skip
❌ Long paragraphs → people scan, not read
❌ "Synergies", "leverage", "circle back" → corporate speak, immediately filtered
❌ Multiple links → spam trigger
❌ Attachments → spam trigger
❌ "Just checking in" follow-ups → annoying, low conversion
❌ Sending Friday afternoon, Monday morning, or after 6 PM → low open rate

---

## What to measure

| Metric | Healthy benchmark |
|---|---|
| Open rate | 35%+ |
| Reply rate | 5-8% |
| Demo booked | 1-2% |
| Meeting → trial | 30-50% |
| Trial → paid | 30-60% |

If any of these is below the benchmark, change ONE thing (subject line, opener, length, CTA) and re-test on the next 50 sends. Don't change everything at once — you won't know what worked.

## Follow-up cadence

Day 0: Send Template B
Day 4: Send 1-line follow-up: "Hey {{first_name}} — bump on this. Worth 15 min next week?"
Day 11: Send Template C
Day 25: Final follow-up with a value-add (a relevant industry stat, a case study link, etc.)

After day 25, drop them. Re-engage in 90 days if you have new product news.
